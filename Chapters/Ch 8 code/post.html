Why I love Rioja

Categories: Spain, Red Wines, Tasting
By James | 13th Aug 2010 | 14 comments

You can't beat the image of a sun-drenched Spanish vineyard for that Hemmingway-esque sense of exotic adventure. The dry, crumbly soil, the distant view of the parched foothills of the Pyrenees, and the faint hum of Spanish fiesta reaching your ears from the local village.

Drinking the wine brings on similar sensations: the rich fruity complexity of a wine which infuses all aspects of Iberian life, and which carries a drop of the bullfighter's spirit in every bottle.
